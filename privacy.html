Privacy Policy

How Zecor collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: June 2026

Who We Are

Zecor is a private, one-on-one hair studio located at 4040 Barrett Dr, Ste 200 Unit 1, Raleigh, NC 27609, operated by Ezequiel. You can reach us at hello@zecorstudio.com or by calling or texting (919) 444-2266.

This privacy policy explains what personal information we collect through this website, why we collect it, and how it is handled.

What Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide through our consultation request form. This may include:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (optional)
  • Your service interest
  • A message describing your hair goals
  • A photo of your natural hair (optional, uploaded at your discretion)

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools that collect personal data. We do not collect any information automatically from visitors who do not submit the form.

Why We Collect It

The information you submit through the consultation request form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and schedule your consultation
  • Prepare for your appointment based on your hair type, goals, and any photos shared
  • Communicate with you regarding your appointment

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond your direct appointment and communication with us.

How Your Information Is Stored

Form submissions are processed through FormBackend, a third-party form service. FormBackend stores your submission securely on their servers and forwards it to our email inbox. You can review FormBackend's privacy practices at formbackend.com/privacy.

Any photos you upload are stored by FormBackend and accessible only to Ezequiel through the FormBackend dashboard. Photos are used exclusively to prepare for your consultation and are not shared with any third party.

We do not store your personal information on our own servers or in any database beyond what FormBackend retains as part of its service.

Who We Share It With

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for commercial purposes. The only parties with access to your submitted information are:

  • Ezequiel — the studio owner, for the purpose of responding to your inquiry
  • FormBackend — as the technical service that processes and stores form submissions

How Long We Keep It

We retain your information only as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was submitted. If you would like your information deleted, simply email us at hello@zecorstudio.com and we will remove it promptly.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of any future communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@zecorstudio.com. We will respond within a reasonable time frame.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our form, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

External Links

Our website contains links to external services including Square (for appointment booking), Instagram, and TikTok. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
